We are sorry to let you go

To delete your account, please send an email request to toptalentapp.id@gmail.com , with this following details:


1. Subject: Request for Account Deletion

2. Verify your account information

Provide your full name, username, and email address associated with the account. This information will help the support team identify and locate your account accurately.

3. Express your intention to delete the account

Clearly state that you wish to delete your account and emphasize that you want all personal data and associated information removed from the company's systems permanently.

4. Provide contact information

Include your current contact information (email address, phone number) so we can reach out to you for further verification or communication regarding your account deletion request.


After 1 to 4x24 hours, we will send you your account deletion confirmation through your contact information and provide an estimated date by which the process will be completed.
